## /dev/null

how do you get /dev/null on windows>?

[http://stackoverflow.com/questions/313111/dev-null-in-windows][dev null in windows]

e.g.

echo 1 > nul
echo 1 > c:\nul
echo 1 > c:\users\nul
echo 1 > c:\windows\nul

or simply just nul

type c:\autoexec.bat > NUL
